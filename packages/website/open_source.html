Open knowledge and open source

Oky brings together girls' health information and technology, and believes sharing this knowledge can make the world a better place.

The technology behind Oky is Open Source and the code can be accessed on request. It's important to Oky to enable more people, not just companies, to understand how technology is contributing to health because health is a public good.

You're invited to take part in opening up the knowledge, by accessing and participating in developing Oky's technology and content. Whether you're just starting out in coding, or are an experienced developer, and you're interested in using the technology or the content behind Oky, we'd love to hear from you. We're of course open to suggestions about how to improve our app, and to building a community around Oky!

To find out more about how you can contribute, or to get access to the project repository on GitHub, contact us.
